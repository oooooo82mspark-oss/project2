Our Mission

Welcome to AnimalFace.AI, your fun and interactive destination for discovering your animal look-alike! Our mission is to combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with lighthearted entertainment to bring a smile to your face.

Who We Are

We are a small team of AI enthusiasts and pet lovers based in Seoul. We created this tool because we were fascinated by the "animal face type" trends in fashion and beauty. We wanted to build a high-quality, privacy-respecting tool that anyone could use to explore their own unique features.

Why We Built This

Unlike many other "quiz" sites that collect user data, we built AnimalFace.AI with a privacy-first approach. By using TensorFlow.js, we ensure that your photos are processed directly on your device and never uploaded to any server. We believe high-quality entertainment shouldn't come at the cost of your personal privacy.

Our Technology

Our tool leverages Google's Teachable Machine technology. We have trained our model on thousands of diverse images to ensure high accuracy and inclusivity. We are constantly updating our database to provide more nuanced results.
