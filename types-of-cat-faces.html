The Allure of the "Cat Face": Sharp & Sophisticated

Why the feline look is considered the epitome of chic and cool beauty.

What is a "Cat Face"?

The "Cat Face" (or "Goyangi-sang") is the aesthetic opposite of the puppy look. It is defined by sharpness, elegance, and a hint of mystery. It is a highly coveted look in the fashion world because it photographs exceptionally well.

Key Characteristics

  • Upturned Eyes: The defining trait. The eyes slant slightly upwards at the outer corners, often enhanced by "cat-eye" makeup.
  • Sharp Nose & Jaw: Features are more defined and angular. High cheekbones are common.
  • Thinner Lips: Or a clearly defined cupid's bow, adding to the refined appearance.

Personality Associations

While "dog faces" look friendly, "cat faces" are often perceived as:

  • Sophisticated and classy
  • Independent and confident
  • Mysterious or "Ice Princess/Prince" vibe
  • Trendy and fashion-forward

Celebrity Examples

K-pop idols like Jennie (Blackpink) and Yeji (ITZY), or actors like Anya Taylor-Joy, perfectly embody this style. Their look commands attention and exudes a powerful aura.
