The "Fox Face" (Yeou-sang): Sharpness Meets Charm

Exploring the high-fashion and magnetic allure of the Fox Face type.

What defines a "Fox Face"?

While the "Cat" face is often seen as cold or chic, the "Fox" face is considered more magnetic and seductive. It combines sharp angles with a knowing, playful expression. In modern aesthetics, this is often called the "Baddie" or "Siren" look.

Visual Characteristics

  • Elongated, Upturned Eyes: Similar to cat eyes but often narrower and longer, reaching towards the temples.
  • Sharp, High Nose Bridge: A prominent, straight nose that adds a level of sophistication to the profile.
  • Defined V-Line Jaw: A sharp chin and clear jawline are essential hallmarks of the fox type.

The "Fox" Aura

Characteristics associated with the Fox type include:

  • Intellectual and strategic
  • Mysterious yet magnetic
  • Natural leadership qualities
  • Highly photogenic and expressive

Beauty Guide for Fox Types

Embrace your sharp features with these tips:

Makeup: Smokey eyes or sharp winged eyeliner. Bold red or deep berry lip colors highlight your natural sophistication.

Hairstyles: Straight, sleek hair or high ponytails emphasize your strong jawline and eye shape.
