The Science Behind AI Face Analysis

Is your face type just a myth? Not exactly. While traditional physiognomy (the study of facial features and personality) has historical roots, modern AI uses Computer Vision to identify geometric patterns.

Our model scans over 100 facial landmarks to calculate the ratio of your features. For instance, the distance between your eyes and the angle of your jawline are converted into mathematical vectors. These vectors are then compared to a dataset of thousands of categorized faces to find your most likely match.
