The Pure & Lovely "Rabbit Face": A Deep Dive

Why the "Rabbit" aesthetic is the gold standard for pure and innocent beauty.

Defining the Rabbit Face (Tokki-sang)

The Rabbit face type is one of the most beloved aesthetics in modern pop culture. It sits between the "Dog" and "Cat" types, combining the friendliness of a puppy with a unique, bright energy that is entirely its own. It is characterized by small, delicate features and an "active" expression.

Key Visual Landmarks

  • Bright, Round Eyes: Often sparkling and alert, giving an impression of curiosity and intelligence.
  • Short Philtrum & Prominent Front Teeth: A slightly shorter space between the nose and lips, often combined with cute front teeth, creates that iconic "bunny" look when smiling.
  • Clear, Fair Skin: This type is often associated with a clean, dewy complexion that emphasizes youthfulness.

The "Rabbit" Personality Profile

Beyond looks, those with rabbit features are often seen as:

  • Highly energetic and social
  • Intuitive and quick-witted
  • Possessing a "natural aegyo" (charming personality)
  • The life of the party but with a soft heart

Styling Tips for Rabbit Types

If you test as a Rabbit type, try these styles:

Makeup: Focus on "Peach" or "Pink" tones. Glossy lips and emphasized under-eye "fat" (aegyo-sal) work best.

Fashion: Soft knits, pastel colors, and ruffles complement your delicate features perfectly.
